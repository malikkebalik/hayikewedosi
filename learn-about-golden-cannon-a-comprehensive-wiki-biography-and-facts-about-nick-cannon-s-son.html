Golden Cannon, welcomed into the world on February 21, 2017, is a renowned celebrity child from California who has captured the hearts of many. Golden Cannon: Bio and Profile Summary

Golden Cannon’s Parentage

Golden Cannon is the eldest son of Nick Cannon and his former girlfriend, Brittany Mesa Bell. Brittany, a former model renowned for her title of Miss Arizona USA in 2010 and later crowned as Miss Guam in 2014, shares a deep connection with Nick. The joyful announcement of their impending parenthood was made during an appearance on the show ‘The Breakfast Club’ in November 2016. Their celestial-themed baby shower took place on February 9, 2017, where their excitement was palpable as they shared numerous pictures of Golden Cannon on their respective social media accounts.

Nick and Brittany expressed their elation at her arrival, posting several heartwarming photos of their newborn “best gift ever.” Notable personalities like Eniko Hart and Jordin Sparks congratulated the couple on this joyous occasion. The announcement of Powerful’s pregnancy was made by Bell and her ex-partner Nick in June 2020.

Golden Cannon also has four step-siblings: Moroccan Scott Cannon, Monroe Cannon, Zion Mixolydian Cannon, and Zillion Heir Cannon. Nick shares twins Moroccan and Monroe with his ex-spouse, the iconic singer Mariah Carey. The former couple, who tied the knot on April 30, 2008, welcomed their fraternal twins on April 30, 2011. After six years of marriage, they parted ways in 2014 and finalized their divorce in 2016. Zion and Zillion, Golden’s half-brothers, were born on June 4, 2021. In the same month, Nick Cannon disclosed that he is expecting his seventh child, a son, with model Alyssa Scott. Notably, Nick shares camaraderie with DJ Abby De La Rosa.
