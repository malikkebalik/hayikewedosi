Kellen Enriquez Returns as Harrison for Christmas 2023

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  301 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Young & Restless’ Summer is crushing hard on Chance Chancellor, but there may still be hope for her and Kyle — especially if the character we’re about to see back on our screens has anything to say about it!

While Kyle has been redefining “it’s complicated” with the vixen-ish Audra Charles, mixing pleasure with, if not business, then corporate intrigue and double-crosses, Summer has been making googly eyes at Chance whenever he enters a room.

Summer even showed up at Chance’s hospital bedside when he was shot, which some, including his girlfriend Sharon, found a little disconcerting. Although she isn’t about trying to steal the guy from her mother’s former rival, Summer did allow herself to be talked into attending a concert with him, which is only going to make her crush harder.

That said, Summer is sure to be keenly missing the Christmas traditions she enjoyed with the Abbotts, and the little family she’d established with Kyle and Harrison.

Although the character technically hasn’t gone anywhere, and has been mentioned on a regular basis, we haven’t seen Harrison onscreen since July. But we have exciting news — Kellen Enriquez will return to the canvas as Harrison for Christmas scenes at the Abbott mansion!

Given how much upheaval the young man has had in his life, Harrison could very likely insist that Kyle invite Summer to join them for the festivities, and with a little help from Jack and Diane, he might just get his way. Whether that happens or not, Harrison is possibly the only person that could reunite Summer and Kyle.

Tune in Wednesday December 20 to see Enriquez, along with Beth Maitland’s Traci, as the Abbotts get into the Christmas spirit!
