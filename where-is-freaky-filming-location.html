Where is the 2020 Freaky Filming Location?

The 2020 movie Freaky filming location was filmed in Duluth and Alpharetta, Georgia, USA. And in the movie, we see a high school which to be more precise is Denmark High School located at 645 Mullinax Rd, Alpharetta.
