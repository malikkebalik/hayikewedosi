Anastasia Tatalina got a face and eye injury in the Olympic Games. She was seen with red tape on her face. 

Tatalina is a Russian freestyle skier competing in the Olympic Games Beijing 2022. She competes at national and international levels and won several medals and accolades. She represented Russia in the World Championships 2017.

Anastasia participated in the 2018 Winter Olympics and is now back at the 2022 Winter Olympics. She scored over 90 points in two consecutive jumps and gold in women's ski big air at the 2021 World Championships. 

Anastasia Tatalina Face And Eye Injury Update

Anastasia Tatalina seemed to have a face and eye injury during the recent Winter Olympic event. She was seen with red tape on her face, which was covering an eye injury.

People initially assumed that it was a cold-weather balaclava with a design on a single side. Her face and eye injury looked concerning, and the tape looked like physio tape.
