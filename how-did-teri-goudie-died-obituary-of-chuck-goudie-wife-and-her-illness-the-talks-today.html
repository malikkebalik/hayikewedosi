Who was Teri Goudie, the wife of Chuck Goudie? Her life story

Teri Goudie was the CEO of Goudie Media Services, a company in Illinois that taught people how to work in the media.

She has been a reporter for ABC News since 1979. After she worked as a broadcast reporter in a medium-sized city for a few years, the company made her a producer.

She was one of the first producers to work with Oprah and help out in real emergencies.

She also made the program that made the news show on WLS-Chicago TV the most watched news show in the country.
