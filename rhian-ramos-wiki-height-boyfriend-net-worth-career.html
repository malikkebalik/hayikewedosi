How did Rhian Ramos start her Professional Career?

Rhian Ramos began her work creating advertisements for food and beverage businesses. She first made an appearance in a branding advertisement for McDonald’s. In the 2006 television drama “Mars Ravelo’s Captain Barbell,” she made her acting début.

She then proceeded to use every opportunity to showcase her acting skills. In 2007, she appeared in her first movie as an actress. “The Promise” was her debut film. She won a Golden Screen Award for her outstanding performance in her debut movie.

Rhian has appeared in several comedic shows on the GMA Network. “Bubble Gang,” “Saturday Badoo,” “My Daddy is a Vampire,” and other comedic shows are some of her works.
