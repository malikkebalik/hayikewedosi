Selena Gomez just showed us what our go-to fall outfit will be and the best part is, you can shop her cozy sweater for under $200. The 30-year-old was out to eat at Carbone in NYC on Oct. 19, when she rocked a baggy, black and white turtleneck sweater with light-wash jeans.

Selena wore a black and white striped Everlane The Cloud Turtleneck that retails for $165 and is available in sizes XXS to XXL. She styled the cozy top with a pair of baggy, light-wash blue, straight-leg Re/Done High Rise Crop Jeans. On top of her sweater, she donned a black Mango Double-Breasted Wool Tailored Coat draped over her shoulders and tied her look together with black leather Reformation Nylah Boots, gold Jennifer Fisher Hollow Kate Hoops, and a Louis Vuitton over the Moon Bag.

As for her glam, Sels slicked back her jet-black hair into a low, middle-parted bun and added a bright red lip for a fun burst of color. Selena is always mixing and matching affordable pieces with high-end pieces, which is what we love about her. Aside from this look, she recently wore a pastel patterned, V-neck Charo Ruiz Iraso Long Dress with a pair of $162 Missoma Lucy Williams Gold Medium Ridge Hoop Earrings.

Another one of our favorite recent sweaters from Selena was her Farm Rio Sunset Stripes Crochet Sweater. The high-neck sheer sweater featured cinched wrists and a waistband and it costs $225. As if her fall outfits couldn’t get any better, she recently wore an oversized, powder-blue turtleneck 3.1 Phillip Lim Drop Shoulder Sweater with a pair of $58 Tnaction Tnabutter Atmosphere Hi-Rise Cropped Leggings from Aritzia, styled with a pair of chunky black patent leather Alexander McQueen Wander Loafers.
