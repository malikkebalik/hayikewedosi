Biography

Lupe Fuentes was born in Cali, Colombia on January 27, 1987. House music DJ and producer whose original, Latin flavor productions are heavily bass-driven and dark. Her single “Drop The Beat” is scheduled to be released on house music legend Todd Terry’s label In House in July of 2014. In 2014 she collaborated with rapper Darryl McDaniels on the song “Get Down.” She grew up in less than meager surroundings as a child, often relying on hand-me-downs and charity from local churches.
