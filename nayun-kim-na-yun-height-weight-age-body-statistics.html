Nayun is a South Korean singer and is best known for being the vocalist and sub-rapper of the South Korean girl group Momoland which was created in 2016 via the reality show, Finding Momoland. The group has a strong fan base and has released several tracks, EPs, and albums such as Welcome to Momoland, Freeze!, Great!, Fun to the World, JJan! Koong! Kwang!, Wonderful Love, I’m So Hot, and Show Me and has also won several awards such as Asia Artist Award, Gaon Chart Music Award, Golden Disc Award, Japan Gold Disc Award, Genie Music Award, Mnet Asian Music Award, Melon Music Award, and Seoul Music Award.
