Karrine Steffans tweeted yesterday (October 11) that she was about to watch the Lil Wayne sex tape that is being shopped.

“The homie @LilTunechi has a sex tape? I know that shit is short than a mugg… *presses play*,” she says.

Steffans, who is also known as Superhead, had a long-standing relationship with Lil Wayne and said during an interview with DJ Vlad that when she was married, she would leave any time that the New Orleans rapper would call her.
