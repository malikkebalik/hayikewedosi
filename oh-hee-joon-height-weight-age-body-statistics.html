Oh Hee-joon Facts

  • In 2013, he was seen as Hyung-jun in the horror drama film Sonyeo.
  • He played the character named Oh Kyung-pyo, Chan-hong’s best friend, in the coming-of-age romantic drama television series How to Buy a Friend in 2020. Oh Hee-joon co-starred in the show alongside Lee Shin-young, Shin Seung-ho, Kim So-hye, Min Do-hee, and Jo Yi-hyun.
  • In 2020, Oh Hee-joon was cast in the supporting role of Do Gwang-soo, Ah-rin’s manager, in the romantic drama television series titled Was It Love?, starring Song Ji-hyo, Son Ho-jun, Song Jong-ho, Kim Min-jun, Koo Ja-sung, and Kim Da-som.
  • He brought to life the character of Min Sang-shik in the romantic comedy television series More Than Friends in 2020.
  • He appeared as Koo Dae-kwon, Shax’s dedicated manager who is overworked between managing the group and Ryok’s acting career, in the television drama series Imitation in 2021.
