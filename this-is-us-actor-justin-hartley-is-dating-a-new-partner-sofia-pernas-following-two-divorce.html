This Is Us Actor, Justin Hartley Is Dating a New Partner, Sofia Pernas Following Two Divorce

Justin Hartley is an American actor who received recognition after starring on the NBC daytime soap opera Passions, as Nicholas Foxworth Crane from 2002 to 2006. Also, he is famous for his roles in the TV series, Smallville on The CW and Adam Newman in The Young and the Restless on CBS.

As of now, he's starring in the NBC drama series This Is Us, as Kevin Pearson. Today we're exploring the interesting details about him in the following sections. 

Justin Hartley's Dating New Girlfriend, Sofia Pernas

Justin Hartley, 44, has found a new woman in his life despite having been divorced twice. As of now, he is reportedly dating his new partner, Sofia Pernas, a Moroccan actress. She now stars in the CBS series Blood & Treasure.

Their dating rumours glittered apparently began in May 2020. They first met during the shooting of The Young and the Restless in April 2015. Then they even shared the screen from 2014 to 2016. After Justin and Lindsay's divorce was finalized, they first spotted sharing a kiss on May 28, 2020, according to TMZ.

However, the former costars had kept their affairs away from the media or spotlight until they shared photos of each other on social media. In the New Year of 2021, the Smallville star shared a picture of himself and Sofia, writing: "Last minutes of 2020 here in LA. Bring on 2021! Happy New Year!".

He is a Twice Married Man

Before Pernas, the Revenge alum has been in a marital relationship with two beautiful wives. He first tied the knot with American actress, Lindsay Hartley, on May 1, 2004, in a private ceremony.

They became engaged on November 13, 2003, following six months of the relationship. They first met on the set of Passion. However, they announced their split on 6 May 2012, citing "irreconcilable differences". Together, they shared a daughter, Isabella Justice Hartley, born on July 3, 2004.

Then on October 28, 2017, he got hitched with American actress, Chrishell Stause, after three years of love affairs. But in Nov. 2019, Justin filed for divorce in Los Angeles Superior Court and listed their separation as July 8 after being married for two years. 

How Much is Justin Hartley Worth?

In early 2021, Justin Hartle has a whopping net worth of $7 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has earned his worth throughout his two decades of an acting career. 

As per his salary, Hartley had previously made $125,000 per episode but now he's making $250,000 per episode from the series, This Is Us. It stars an ensemble cast including Alexandra Breckenridge as Sophie, Mandy Moore as Rebecca Pearson, Milo Ventimiglia as Jack Pearson, and others.

His other significant TV work includes Passions, Smallville, Gemini Division, Emily Owens, M.D., Mistresses, The Young and the Restless, and Jane the Virgin. Further, he has acted in films such as Spring Breakdown, A Bad Moms Christmas, Little, Jexi, and The Hunt.

Apart, he and his divorced wife Chrishell bought the 6,700 square feet home at a price of $4.65 million in Encino, California.

Bio and Early Life

The 1.89 m tall Justin Hartley was born on January 29, 1977, in Galesburg, Illinois, United States. He is the son of Mark D Hartley and Jackie Hartley. His older brother is Nathan Hartley and his sisters are Megan Hartley and Gabriela Carrillo.

Hartley studied at the Carl Sandburg High School and Southern Illinois University Carbondale. Then he enrolled at the University of Illinois at Chicago where he completes his graduation in theatre and history.
