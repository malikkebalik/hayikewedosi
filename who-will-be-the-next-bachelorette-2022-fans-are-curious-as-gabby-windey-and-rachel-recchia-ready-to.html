As the Bachelors Season 26 came to an end, people were curious to know, Who will be the next bachelorette in 2022.

Bachelorette has always been a single lady venturing to find her love, but a twist is there as both Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia will be in the Bachelorette Season 19.

This twist made all the viewers surprised by it. Both the ladies were supportive of each other till the end and were eliminated in the finale of the Bachelors Season 26.

Who Will Be The Next Bachelorette 2022?

Both Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia are the next Bachelorette in 2022 and were announced at the Clayton's After The Final Rose special episode.

Many viewers were hoping that Gabby will be announced as the next bachelorette. But both Gabby and Rachel were the next bachelorette which was a twist that nobody saw it coming.

The viewer was happy that the two ladies will be getting their happy endings in the bachelorette. Their journey on the Bachelors Season 26 was not smooth but was filled with tears and heartbreaks as the bachelor Clayton had given his heart to not one but three ladies.

But in the second half of the finale of the Bachelors, Clayton broke up with both Gabby and Rachel as he chose Susie as his lover. Many viewers were mad at how awful his decision was and how he had broken the hearts of the two beautiful ladies.

Meet the Next Bachelorette Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia 

Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia are the contestants of Bachelors Season 26 and the next Bachelorettes on Season 19. Gabby was born and raised in Illinois.

She attended college in Colorado and has worked as an ICU nurse. She was also an NFL cheerleader for the Denver Broncos but left in April 2021, while Rachel is from Clermont, Florida.

Rachel has started to work as a commercial pilot and flight instructor after earning her private pilot license. She is a Harry Potter fan and claims herself as a Potterhead.
