Who is Raviv Ullman?

Raviv Ullman is an Israeli-American actor, director, and musician.

Likely, he is well known for playing Phil Diffy, the main character in the Disney Channel series Phil of the Future.

Raviv Ullman: Age, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

Raviv Ullman was born in Eilat, Israel on January 24, 1986. His father’s name is  Brian Ullman and his mother’s name is Laura Ullman.

He has two siblings namely, Nadav Ullman, and Tali Ullman. Raviv holds American-Israeli citizenship and Ashkenazi Jewish ethnicity. His birth sign is  Aquarius.

His role in the play ‘A Rosen by Any Other Name. He made his TV debut with a supporting role in the TV movie ‘Growing Up Brady’ in 2000.
