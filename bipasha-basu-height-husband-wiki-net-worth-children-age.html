Who Is Bipasha Basu? - Bipasha Basu Wikipedia

Bipasha Basu Wiki:- Bipasha Basu is an Indian actress. She was born on 7 January 1979 in New Delhi, India. Bipasha who mainly works in Hindi films has also appeared in Tamil, Telugu, Bengali and English films. Basu debuted in Vijay Galani's Ajnabee in 2001. She starred in Raaz, the most successful horror flick of 2002. For Raaz, she received a nomination for the Filmfare Award's Best Actress category. Basu appeared in the sensual thriller Jism (2003), directed by Pooja Bhatt, which did well at the box office and garnered positive reviews from reviewers. Zameen, her subsequent release, did not resonate well with viewers.

No Entry, Corporate, and Dhoom 2's success in 2005 and 2006 solidified Basu's position as a prominent actress. In 2006, she starred in four big films that did well both critically and commercially: Phir Hera Pheri, Corporate, Omkara, and Dhoom 2. Dum Maaro Dum, her lone 2011 release, received positive reviews from reviewers and did fairly well at the box office. In 2015, Basu starred alongside her future husband Karan Singh Grover in Bhushan Patel's romantic horror thriller Alone.
