Biography

Eliza Cummings was born in England on January 25, 1991. A cover model for i-D magazine and Dazed & Confused, she is also notable for her runway work for DKNY and Anna Sui, as well as for her advertising campaigns for Yves Saint Laurent and Calvin Klein. She appeared with film actor Vincent Cassel in an advertisement for the men’s cologne L’Homme de Nuit. She signed with Select Model Management when she was only fifteen years old and soon thereafter appeared in a Vogue Italia photo spread.
