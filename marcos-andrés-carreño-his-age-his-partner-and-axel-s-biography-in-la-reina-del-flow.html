MARCOS ANDRS CARREO: HIS AGE, HIS PARTNER AND AXELS BIOGRAPHY IN LA REINA DEL FLOW

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  486 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

When it comes to series, one of the most successful premieres that the Netflix platform has had in recent times has been  ‘La reina del flow’ . The premiere of a fiction that conquered everyone with its first installment, 82 heart-stopping episodes that left everyone wanting more. A Colombian series that has crossed borders after landing on the Netflix platform and where we were able to learn about the love story of Charly Flow, played by Carlos Torres , and Yeimi Montoya, played by Carolina Ramírez , along with actors like Marcos Andrés Carreño.

In this story of love, betrayals and a lot of flow, Axel was introduced to us as one of the great protagonists of the plot. A character who, without a doubt, did not leave anyone indifferent and who shone with his own light in numerous scenes of fiction. For this reason, and given that he has become one of the artists of the moment, we will address a little more in depth about the actor of this popular series,  a man originally from Colombia who has a completely brilliant professional career. You’ll be surprised!

YOUR PROFESSIONAL CAREER

Marcos Andrés Carreño is an artist of Colombian origin who is known in the world of music under the alias Kiño , a man who, before dedicating himself to the world of acting, already had an established base in the music industry. He started his career in the early 90s, but his official release did not come until 1999, the date on which he would create the group RH Klandestino. It was then that he became known nationally with songs like ‘Don’t laugh’ and ‘On your screen’.

Since mid-2000, Marcos Andrés Carreño decided to create a record label and a recording studio , which he named K-Music. With this new project, the artist managed to expand his career as a producer and signed new and established talents from the local scene. In addition, with this label he has produced more than fifteen albums for essential Colombian Rap artists and has worked with stars such as Nicky Jam.

Throughout his career as a soloist, the interpreter has released four recording projects, two mixtapes and one EP. In addition, he has been an actor, screenwriter and musician in films, short films and series that have given him great recognition among the public, including the novel ‘Rosario Tijeras’ in 2010 and ‘La reina del Flow’ in 2018. Currently, he is a contestant on the culinary reality show ‘BakeOff Colombia’.

HIS PERSONAL LIFE

On a personal level, Marcos Andrés Carreño shows himself to be a faithful follower of social networks. In fact, he has more than 512,000 followers on his Instagram account, a platform where he also shares his closest and most intimate face. In addition, he has shown that, without counting the interpretation, he is passionate about the sport.

On the other hand, in the field of love, the actor  maintains a sentimental relationship with María Paulina Restrepo,  a young woman who is passionate about the world of pastry and with whom she shares tender moments on social networks.
