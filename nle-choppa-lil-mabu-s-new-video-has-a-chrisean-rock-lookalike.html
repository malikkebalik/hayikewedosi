NLE Choppa and Lil Mabu have released a new video for their track, “Shotta Flow,” which stars a video vixen who bears more than a passing resemblance to Chrisean Rock.

The video, which was released on Friday (February 23), is a remix of “Shotta Flow 7,” and the use of a Rock lookalike in the clip appears to be a dig at Chrisean’s baby’s daddy, Blueface.

Both artists have a history of antipathy with Blueface.

NLE’s beef began when Blueface allegedly hit on Choppa’s baby’s mother Marissa Da’Nae, while Lil Mabu’s started when he and Rock began hooking up.
