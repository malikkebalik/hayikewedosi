Biography

Luke Steele was born in Perth, Australia on December 13, 1979. One-half of the electronic duo Empire of the Sun, the group known for the hit album Walking on a Dream, who also founded the alternative rock band The Sleepy Jackson. He has cited Cole Porter, Kill And The Gang, and The Cure among his musical influences. He formed The Sleepy Jackson in 1998 and, on the verge of a record deal with EMI, his bandmates quit, forcing him to reform the group from scratch.
