Who Is Shaquille O’Neal?

Shaquille O'Neal is a former basketball player who now works as a sports analyst. His 19-year commitment to his basketball profession is what has made him famous. He was a six-time NBA All-Star and one of the game's all-time greats. Shaq has also published four rap albums, as well as various TV shows and films, in addition to basketball. When he decided to try basketball, he rapidly became a celebrity. This also provided him the opportunity to pursue other opportunities in the film and rap industries. O'Neal was a member of the 1996 Olympic basketball squad that won gold. During his basketball career, he revealed his intention to join the Los Angeles Lakers, as part of a seven-year $121 million contract.
