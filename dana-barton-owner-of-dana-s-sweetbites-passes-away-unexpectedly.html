Dana Barton, owner of Danas SweetBites passes away unexpectedly

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  511 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Mooresville, NC –The unexpected passing of Dana Barton, confirmed on Monday, October 30, 2023, has cast a shadow of sorrow over the hearts of those who had the privilege of knowing him. Remembered with deep affection by all who were touched by his presence, Dana’s untimely departure has evoked a heartfelt outpouring of condolences and tributes from friends and the entire community. In this trying time, the community comes together to offer their support and solace to Dana Barton’s grieving family.

Yusuf Benallal posted on Facebook,

“Dana, How can I tell one of the most beautiful souls I’ve ever loved how much I admire them in a few words. I’ve have so many memories that bring me so much happiness then cancer reminds me how it’s changed all our life’s. You were so strong and so brave Dana.

“You’ve taught me so much about patience and forgiveness and resilience. It makes me so sad such an amazing person like yourself was tested the way you were. I knew how much you wanted to change and live a purposeful life. You have inspired me so much Dana and I will forever be grateful that I got to know you and love you. All the amazing times we had. All the collinktions and meeting at emilies not Amelie’s.

“My family loves you dearly and they are so saddened. I love you Dana forever and always. You will always be apart of me because you have had one of the biggest positive impacts on my life. As far as I see it you beat cancer. You showed up and showed out everyday even when you were thinking otherwise. God has taken you to the highest Heaven were there is no pain and suffering. I know you’re looking down on us waiting for us to reunite inshallah. I’ll miss you Dana. I love you so much. RIP my danedane. Dana Barton”

As the community waits for further information surrounding the circumstances of Dana Barton’s passing, including the specific cause of his death, a sense of anticipation hangs in the air. The family intends to release Dana Barton’s obituary and details of funeral arrangements, providing an opportunity for friends and loved ones to come together and pay their respects to a cherished individual whose memory will forever hold a special place in their hearts.
