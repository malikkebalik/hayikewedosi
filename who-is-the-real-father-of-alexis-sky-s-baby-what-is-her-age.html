Who is the real father of Alexis Skys baby? What is her age?

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  412 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

In a surprising revelation, Instagram model Alexis Skyy has unveiled Brandon Medford as the biological father of her daughter, Alaiya Grace, after years of speculations involving rapper Fetty Wap. Alexis, known for her candid sharing on social media, disclosed this new information and shed light on their co-parenting challenges.

The story behind their connection goes back to 2017 when Alexis met Brandon Medford, a luxury car dealer in Los Angeles. However, she claims that it wasn’t until she purchased a car from him that he agreed to take a paternity test to determine his relationship with Alaiya Grace.

The paternity test results confirmed Brandon as the father, bringing an end to the uncertainty that had clouded their daughter’s parentage. Alexis also accuses Brandon of selling her a car without license plates, which she perceives as a dishonest act.

Adding another layer to the situation, Alexis suggests that Brandon is ashamed of their daughter, particularly due to her disability. In response, Brandon Medford, while speaking on “The Kitchen Talk Podcast,” explained that the paternity test request initially came through a private conversation. He noted that it took a week to receive the results.

When questioned about the delay in requesting the test, Brandon admitted he didn’t have an answer. The situation has left many surprised, and some have humorously questioned the need for Maury Povich, the well-known TV host of “The Maury Show” specializing in paternity testing, to intervene in such matters.

The revelation not only clarifies the identity of Alaiya Grace’s father but also highlights the challenges of co-parenting that Alexis Skyy faces with Brandon Medford. Their open discussions about these struggles bring attention to the importance of effective communication and cooperation in raising their child.
