St. Charles MO Community College alumnus Brandon Szuba dies suddenly: obituary

 Posted on August 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  296 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The St. Charles, MO community is in mourning after the untimely passing of Brandon Szuba, an esteemed alumnus of St. Charles Community College. His sudden demise has left friends and acquaintances in shock and disbelief, evident from a poignant Facebook post expressing heartfelt condolences and love for Brandon.

The circumstances surrounding Brandon Szuba’s passing have not been disclosed, leaving many in the community deeply saddened by the loss of this cherished individual.

Brandon Szuba, a dedicated son and brother, not only deeply impacted those closest to him but also left an enduring impression on everyone fortunate enough to cross paths with him. His familial relationships were the cornerstone of his character, demonstrating unwavering respect and gratitude toward his parents for their sacrifices in providing a nurturing environment. His siblings recall his brotherly affection and willingness to offer support whenever needed.

Haley Perez posted on Facebook,

“Thank you for the memories. The good. The bad. The in between. Have a beer with our girl Catie
My condolences & prayers to all of Brandon’s close friends and family during this difficult time.
May you rest in peace Brandon Szuba
I’ll see you on the other side”

Despite his numerous accomplishments, Brandon remained remarkably humble, attributing his successes to the collective efforts of others and the support he received. This humility garnered affection from colleagues, friends, and acquaintances alike.

Brandon’s approachability was a standout trait, described as someone easy to collaborate with, open to new ideas, and dedicated to collective efforts for the greater good. His sympathetic nature shone through in conversations, offering solace and guidance to those seeking assistance.

The family intends to release Brandon Szuba’s obituary, allowing the community to honor and celebrate the life of a remarkable individual known for his humility and compassionate nature.
