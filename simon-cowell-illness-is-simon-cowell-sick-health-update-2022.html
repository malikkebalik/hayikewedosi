Simon Cowell’s Personal Life

Cowell married Idol makeup artist Mezhgan Hussainy in 2010 after dating entertainment reporter Terri Seymour. The following year, however, the couple called off their engagement, with Cowell stating, “We came to the conclusion that I’m a hopeless boyfriend.” He afterward began dating actress and model Carmen Electra.

By 2013, the famous TV personality was secretly dating Lauren Silverman, a New York City socialite who was then married to a friend of Cowell’s. Silverman’s husband sought a divorce after their romance resulted in a pregnancy. Cowell became a father on February 14, 2014, when his son Eric was born.

Despite previously claiming that he had no desire for children, Cowell had changed his mind by the time his own son was born. He told the media, “Eric is simply wonderful and so humorous.” “Becoming a father is the most wonderful thing that has ever occurred to me.”

Cowell announced in 2019 that he has switched to a vegan diet in order to live a healthier lifestyle.
